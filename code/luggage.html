Luggage Policies

Welcome aboard our airline. Here are the luggage policies for different travel classes:

Economy Class

In Economy Class, each passenger is allowed to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item (such as a purse or laptop bag). The maximum weight limit for the carry-on bag is 7 kg (15 lbs).

Checked luggage is available for an additional fee. Passengers in Economy Class can check one bag with a maximum weight of 23 kg (50 lbs).

Business Class

Business Class passengers are allowed to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item. The maximum weight limit for the carry-on bag is 10 kg (22 lbs).

Complimentary checked luggage is included in Business Class. Passengers can check two bags, each with a maximum weight of 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag.

First Class

First Class passengers enjoy generous luggage allowances. They can bring one carry-on bag and one personal item. The maximum weight limit for the carry-on bag is 15 kg (33 lbs).

Complimentary checked luggage is included in First Class. Passengers can check three bags, each with a maximum weight of 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag.
